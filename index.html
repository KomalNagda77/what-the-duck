The Story

For years, humans have underestimated ducks. "Oh, look at them, waddling around, minding their own business," they said.

Fools.

Beneath those innocent beady eyes and flappy feet lies a highly sophisticated secret society—The Webbed Order. Their leader, the infamous Duke Quackington III, has had enough of our bread-based bribery and casual pond invasions.

They are fighting back.

Strange things are happening:

  • People are waking up to find their shoes filled with pond water.
  • Mysterious "quack" sounds are being heard through radio frequencies at odd hours.
  • GPS devices are mysteriously rerouting humans straight into duck-infested lakes.

The media refuses to report on it. The government is silent.

But you? You know something’s up.

There is only one clue—the first breadcrumb in a long trail of secrets. A rogue duck, who defected from the Order, has left it behind. But be careful—the ducks don’t like snitches.

The game has already begun. The ducks are watching. The first clue is right under your beak.
